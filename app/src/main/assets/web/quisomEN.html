WHO ARE WE?

Jovalma is a non-profit cooperative that is aimed at the youth group, offering a service for the prevention of mental disorders and the promotion of good mental and physical health thanks to a group of professionals committed to improving quality of life of this youth.

The work team has the necessary experience and training in the youth group to be able to get this youth healthy habits.

Our vision of the future

We want to be a reference in the provision of services on prevention in mental health in young people. Therefore, Jovalma wants to offer a service of the highest quality to improve the mental well-being of young people with the participation of the families, professionals and institutions involved.

We show you our values

Quality, responsibility, honesty, teamwork, creativity, innovation, empathy, assertiveness, etc.

Will of service to young people, human treatment, transparency and empathy in the actions, confidentiality, objectivity in the decisions, economic balance, respect for the environment, recognition of the professionals who work for the cooperative and those who collaborate with this Fostering equal opportunities for any participant, both workers, users and collaborators.

We achieve our responsibilities

  • We offer professionalism in the treatment and attention to our youth involved, as well as the professionals and relatives involved.

  • Responsibility for the company to maintain quality in its services and products. For this, collaborations will be made with educational and psycho-psychoanalytical centers to offer a broad service to the user.

  • Responsibility for the environment, optimizing the maximum resources used and minimizing the maximum different energy.

  • Responsibility regarding the workplace, by means of good recruitment to the professionals involved and offering measures to reconcile work and family.
